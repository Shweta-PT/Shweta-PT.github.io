About Me

A quick background on my educational and professional journey so far.

My name is Shweta Priya Toppo and I am a dynamic and driven individual with a diverse background that spans across engineering, software testing, and international marketing. Originally from Bangalore, India, I completed my Bachelor's degree in Instrumentation and Control Engineering in 2016, which served as a foundation for my technical expertise.
After graduation, I joined Accenture as a software engineer, where I worked as a functional tester for 3.5 years. During this time, I gained valuable experience in software development, quality assurance, and project management. However, seeking a new challenge and a change of scenery, I decided to pursue an MBA in International Marketing in Berlin, Germany.
While studying, I also worked as a working student QA, which allowed me to apply my technical skills in a practical setting while learning new skills in marketing and project management. Through this experience, I learned to multitask and manage my time effectively, demonstrating my adaptability and versatility.
In November 2022, I successfully completed my MBA and was offered a full-time position in the company I worked for as a working student. Now, I am eager to expand my professional horizons and seek new opportunities to further develop my skills and expertise. With a strong foundation in engineering, software development, and marketing, I am well-positioned to thrive in a variety of roles and industries, bringing a unique perspective and valuable skills to any organization.
